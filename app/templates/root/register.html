PORT-HARCOURT
CARNIRIV

28-29 December, 2024. Yakubu Gowon Stadium, Port-Harcourt. Rivers State.

About The Event

The Port-Harcourt Carniriv is an annual festival hosted to promote peace and oneness among the diverse people of Rivers State. it involves, sport competitions with quality prices to be won, Band Dance, Pageantry, Awards, Red Carpet and Night show featuring performance from top celebrities across Africa. it is indeed a concert not to be missed.

Where

Yakubu Gowon Stadium, Elekahia, Port Harcourt.

When

28-29 December, 2024.
