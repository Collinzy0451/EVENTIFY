Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to some of the common questions about Eventify

What is the registration process?

The registration process is simple. Click on the 'Register Now' button, fill in the required details, and submit the form.

Can I cancel my registration?

Yes, you can cancel your registration up to 24 hours before the event. Please contact our support team for assistance.

Is there a deadline for registration?

Yes, each event has a specific registration deadline which will be mentioned on the event page.

Can I register multiple people for an event?

Yes, you can register multiple attendees. Please fill out a registration form for each person attending.

Can I participate in the event remotely?

Some events may offer remote participation options such as live streaming or webinars. Refer to the event details for more information.

How do I provide feedback about an event I attended?

You can provide feedback through the feedback form provided after the event or by contacting the event organizers directly.
